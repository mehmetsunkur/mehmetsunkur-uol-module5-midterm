Researchers create AI systems that play games for several reasons, including:

* **To test and advance AI algorithms:** Games provide a challenging and controlled environment to develop and evaluate new AI techniques, such as reinforcement learning and Monte Carlo tree search.

* **To understand human intelligence:** By studying how AI agents learn to play games at a high level, researchers can gain insights into the nature of human decision-making and problem-solving.
* **To create entertaining and engaging experiences:** Game-playing AI can be used to create new forms of entertainment, such as challenging opponents in video games or interactive storytelling experiences.
