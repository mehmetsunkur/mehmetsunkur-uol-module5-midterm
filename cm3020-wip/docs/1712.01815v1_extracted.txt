Researchers create AI systems that play games for a few reasons:

* **To test and advance AI algorithms:** Games provide complex, rule-based environments where AI agents can learn and improve their decision-making abilities. Success in games like chess or Go demonstrates the power of these algorithms.

* **To explore fundamental questions about intelligence:** By studying how AI learns to play games, researchers gain insights into human cognition, learning, and problem-solving.

* **To develop applications beyond gaming:** The techniques developed for game-playing AI often have broader applications in fields like robotics, autonomous driving, and resource optimization.
