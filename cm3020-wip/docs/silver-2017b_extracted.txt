Researchers create AI systems that play games for a few reasons:

* **To test and advance AI algorithms:** Games provide complex, rule-based environments where AI agents can learn and improve their decision-making abilities. Success in games like chess or Go demonstrates the power of these algorithms.

* **To explore general intelligence:** By training AI to master games with vastly different rules and strategies, researchers hope to gain insights into the nature of intelligence itself and develop more adaptable AI systems.
* **For fun and entertainment:** Creating AI that can play games at a superhuman level is a fascinating challenge and can lead to entertaining applications.
