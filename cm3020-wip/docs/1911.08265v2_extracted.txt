Researchers create AI systems that play games for several reasons:

* **To advance our understanding of intelligence:** Games provide complex, rule-based environments where AI agents can learn and demonstrate intelligent behavior such as planning, strategy, and adaptation.

* **To develop new algorithms and techniques:** The challenges posed by game playing often lead to the development of novel algorithms and techniques that can be applied to other domains, such as robotics, autonomous driving, and drug discovery.
* **To benchmark progress in AI:** Games provide a standardized platform for comparing the performance of different AI agents and tracking progress in the field.

* **For entertainment and education:** Game-playing AI agents can provide engaging and educational experiences for humans.
