Researchers create AI systems that play games for several reasons, including:

* **To develop and test new reinforcement learning algorithms:** Games provide a controlled environment where researchers can experiment with different algorithms and evaluate their performance.

* **To understand how intelligence emerges:** By studying how AI agents learn to play games, researchers can gain insights into the nature of intelligence and decision-making.
* **To train AI agents for real-world tasks:** Games can be used as a testbed for training AI agents that can perform complex tasks in real-world environments, such as controlling robots or managing financial portfolios.
