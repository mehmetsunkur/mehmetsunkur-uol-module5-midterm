The text explains that AI systems are trained to play games for several reasons, including:

* **Improving AI Capabilities:** Playing games requires complex decision-making, strategy development, and adaptation to changing environments. These are all skills that can be transferred to other areas of AI development.
* **Benchmarking Progress:** Games provide a standardized environment where AI performance can be measured and compared. This helps researchers track progress in the field.
